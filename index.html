Who We Are
Negah Baluchestan is a plastic shopping bag manufacturing, virgin granules exporter and wholesale company.

plastic sheeting, plastic shrink-wrap, Form Fill and Seal, pallet wrap, drop sheets, (gravure and flexo, up to 8 colours)
refuse bags, bubble wrap, polypropylene bags and agricultural products. We don’t only keep stock of many items but manufacture
to your exact specifications and requirements
